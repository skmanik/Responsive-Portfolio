About Me

Why hello there, my name is Sunny and I'm a recent UC Davis graduate majoring in English Literature. Right now I'm learning to code at UC Berkeley's Extension Program, where I hope to transition my love of analysis and written expression from the English language to computer languages!

When I'm not grappling with coding homework, I like to spend my time learning foreign languages (currently: French, and enough Korean to decipher some awesome web comics), drinking chai brewed by my mom (mine is never any good), trekking in the San Jose foothills pretending I'm the despondent heroine of a Jane Austen novel, watching random international dramas on Netflix with my brother (currently: Frankenstein Chronicles with Sean Bean, just to see if he gets killed off as he always manages to do), and using an obnoxious amount of parentheses (sorry).
